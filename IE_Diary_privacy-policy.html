1. Introduction

I/E Diary (the "App") is provided by 3TStone Ltd ("we", "us", or "our"), a company registered in the United Kingdom with a Hong Kong office. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the App.

This Privacy Policy is available in multiple languages. In case of any conflict between versions, the English version shall prevail.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address used for registration and authentication

2.2 Financial Records

  • Income and expense entries, amounts, currencies, categories, notes, timestamps

2.3 Media

  • Photos you attach to transactions (for example, receipts or invoice images)

2.4 Demographic Data

  • Optional information such as age range and general location (city/country) used for anonymized comparisons

2.5 Device and Technical Data

  • Device model, operating system version, IP address, app version, crash logs

2.6 Usage Data

  • Screens visited, feature usage, session length and other analytics events, collected via Google Analytics for Firebase

2.7 Cookies and Similar Technologies

  • Firebase Analytics SDK: Collects anonymous usage data
  • Device Identifiers: Advertising IDs or device IDs for analytics purposes

You can control analytics tracking through your device settings or in-App privacy settings (Settings > Privacy > Analytics)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide core App functionality, including recording transactions, multi-currency calculations, generating reports and charts
  • Authenticate and manage user accounts, including password reset and subscription status
  • Store and display your transaction history and related photos across your devices
  • Generate anonymized and aggregated statistics and comparisons
  • Monitor performance, fix bugs, and improve existing features through analytics
  • Protect the App and our users against fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from authorities where required

3.1 Automated Decision-Making

We do not use your personal data for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you. Some App features use automated processing for convenience (e.g., categorizing transactions), but you can always manually override them.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Where laws such as the GDPR or PIPL apply, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract: To provide the App and its core features
  • Legitimate interests: To secure the App, prevent abuse, and improve services
  • Consent: For optional features such as analytics; you can withdraw consent anytime
  • Legal obligations: To comply with accounting, tax, and regulatory requirements

5. Sharing Your Information

Important: We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
For California residents: We do not "sell" or "share" personal information as defined under CCPA/CPRA.

5.1 Service Providers

Google Firebase Services:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure user login and account management
  • Cloud Firestore: For storing transaction data
  • Firebase Storage: For storing transaction photos
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For crash reporting and app stability
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: For usage analytics (you can opt out in Settings > Privacy > Analytics)

Subject to: Google's Privacy Policy and Firebase Data Processing Terms

Third-party currency rate providers: Which only receive non-personal data (currency codes)

These providers act on our instructions under data processing agreements and must protect your information.

5.2 Legal and Safety

We may disclose information if reasonably necessary to:

  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of our users, the public, or 3TStone Ltd

5.3 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this Privacy Policy.

5.4 International Data Transfer Safeguards

Your information may be processed outside your country, including the United States (Google Firebase servers). We use appropriate safeguards:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Google Firebase Data Processing Terms
  • Additional contractual and technical measures

For China users (PIPL):
Cross-border transfers comply with PIPL Article 38 through standardized contracts or security assessments with regulatory authorities.

For EU/EEA users (GDPR):
Transfers rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and adequacy determinations recognized by the European Commission.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary:

  • Account data: Retained while active. After deletion, basic info (email, user ID) retained for 30 days for recovery, then permanently deleted
  • Transaction data and photos: Stored until you delete them or request account deletion. Upon deletion, immediately removed from active systems and permanently deleted from backups within 90 days
  • Analytics data: Retained in aggregated/pseudonymized form for up to 14 months (Firebase default), then automatically deleted
  • Crash logs: Retained for up to 90 days for debugging, then automatically deleted
  • Legal/compliance data: Where required by law (e.g., tax records), retained for up to 7 years

You may delete individual entries or your entire account at any time in the App (Settings > Account > Delete Account).

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures:

  • Transport Layer Security (HTTPS) for data in transit
  • Access controls and authentication to limit system access
  • Firebase Security Rules to restrict access to your account data
  • Regular monitoring, logging, and review of systems for vulnerabilities
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data stored in Firebase

No system is completely secure, but we work to protect your information.

8.1 Data Breach Notification

In case of a data breach affecting your information, we will:

  • Notify affected users via email or in-App notification within 72 hours (or as required by law)
  • Inform relevant data protection authorities where required
  • Take immediate steps to contain and remediate the breach
  • Provide information about the nature of the breach and protective steps

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Age thresholds vary by jurisdiction:

  • United States: Under 13 years old (COPPA)
  • European Union: Under 16 years old (or lower age set by member states, typically 13-16)

If we discover personal information from a child without appropriate consent, we will delete it as soon as reasonably possible. Contact us at support@3tstone.com if you believe a child has provided us with personal information.
